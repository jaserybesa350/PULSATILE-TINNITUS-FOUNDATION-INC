Pulsatile Tinnitus Foundation Inc

A 501(c)(3) charitable non-profit dedicated to advancing research, providing educational resources, and offering critical support for individuals living with pulsatile tinnitus across the United States.

Our Core Mission & Initiatives

Advancing Pulsatile Tinnitus Research & Support

As a leading charitable foundation for pulsatile tinnitus, we focus on addressing the unmet needs of those affected by this condition through targeted research, education, and community support:

  • Medical Research Funding: Supporting scientific and clinical research to advance the understanding, diagnosis, and treatment of pulsatile tinnitus.
  • Patient Educational Resources: Creating and distributing free, evidence-based educational materials for individuals living with pulsatile tinnitus and their caregivers.
  • Community Support: Fostering a supportive community for pulsatile tinnitus patients to connect, share experiences, and access peer support.
  • Medical Professional Outreach: Educating healthcare providers on the latest advancements in pulsatile tinnitus diagnosis and care to improve patient outcomes.

About Pulsatile Tinnitus Foundation Inc

Our Mission & Charitable Purpose

Established in 2018 and organized under Section 402 of the New York Not-for-Profit Corporation Law, Pulsatile Tinnitus Foundation Inc is a registered 501(c)(3) charitable non-profit with a core mission to advance charitable, educational, and scientific efforts for pulsatile tinnitus.

Headquartered in Ulster County, New York, our foundation is governed by a board of dedicated directors with expertise in medicine, audiology, and non-profit leadership. We are committed to funding impactful research, creating accessible educational resources, and fostering a supportive community for those living with pulsatile tinnitus.

Per our corporate charter, all assets upon dissolution are distributed to other 501(c)(3) organizations advancing hearing health and tinnitus research, and no net earnings inure to the benefit of private individuals.
